## License Compliance Notes

1. **All included models have permissive licenses** (MIT or near-MIT) suitable for commercial use at $0 revenue.
2. **SF3D (Stability Community License)** is acceptable for our $0 revenue project. If revenue exceeds $1M/year, enterprise license required.
3. **Full license texts** are pulled into `sculpt/licenses/` by `scripts/pull_licenses.py` for audit trail.
4. **No GPL or copyleft dependencies** in the dependency chain.
4. **All model weights** are fetched at inference time from HF Spaces — sculpt does not bundle or redistribute weights.
